what is mutual aid?

voluntary, collaborative exchanges of resources and services for common benefit amongst community members to overcome social, economic, and political barriers to meet common needs

 

what is your organization?

a small group of friends and families seeing food insecurity in our community and picking up a soup ladle

 

how can i get involved?

simply meet the need of a neighbor and you are part of the solution, also follow our facebook page to be aware of upcoming opportunities to assist and receive assistance
